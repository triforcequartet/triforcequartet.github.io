Custom
Performances for
Any Occasion

Have an event in mind that’s not on our list? Let’s make it happen.

Not every performance fits neatly into a category. Maybe you’re planning a private surprise for a loved one, hosting a themed dinner, producing a live-streamed event, or curating music for an art opening. Whatever your idea, Triforce Quartet can help bring it to life with a performance designed just for you.

With our versatile repertoire, spanning classical masterpieces, cinematic themes, and video game soundtracks, coupled with our experience performing everywhere from concert halls to dive bars, we’re ready to make your unique vision a reality.

Why Choose Triforce Quartet for a Custom Event?

  • Flexible Repertoire

    We can craft a playlist that’s entirely classical, entirely pop culture, or a creative blend of both.

  • Creative Collaboration

    We work closely with you to understand your concept and make sure our performance enhances the mood, theme, and flow of your event.

  • Any Location

    From large outdoor venues to intimate living rooms, we’ve adapted our performances to fit nearly every kind of space.

  • Professional and Prepared

    You’ll get the same level of polish and attention to detail we bring to corporate events, conventions, and weddings—no matter how small or unusual the booking.

How to Book a Custom Performance

  • Tell Us About Your Event

    Share your vision, date, and location.

  • Collaborate on Details

    We’ll discuss music choices, timing, and any special touches.

  • Enjoy the Show

    Sit back and let us bring your idea to life.

Outside-of-the-box Idea? Challenge accepted.

Let’s level up together and craft something that’s one-of-a-kind. We welcome you to reach out with your own unique ideas, event requests, or vision for how our quartet could musically be part of a moment you are creating. We can talk through details together and score a memorable musical experience for you.
